About İlteksan

Shaping the Future of
Advanced Industry

İlteksan Mühendislik is a leading engineering and technical solutions company specializing in industrial compression, HVAC, cooling, and process systems. We represent the world's most trusted manufacturers across Türkiye and the region.

From initial consultation and equipment supply to commissioning and long-term after-sales support, our team of expert engineers ensures every system performs at peak efficiency throughout its operational life.

With deep expertise across manufacturing, energy, water treatment, and pharmaceuticals, we deliver integrated solutions that drive operational excellence and measurable results.
